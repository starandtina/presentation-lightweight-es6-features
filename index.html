History of ECMAScript

  • Edition 1: June 1997
  • Edition 2: June 1998
  • Edition 3: December 1999 regular expressions, better string handling, new control statements, try/catch exception handling, etc...
  • Edition 4: abandoned on July 2008
  • Edition 5: December 2009 add 'strict mode', getter & setter, JSON, Array, Object, etc...
  • Edition 6: Stabilizing on June 18, 2015
  • Edition 7(work in progress): Async, Object.observe, typed objects, SIMD, etc...
